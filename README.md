# AI Food Project 🍽️🤖

AI Food Project is a web application that allows users to analyze their meals using artificial intelligence.

The application lets users take a photo of their plate and automatically estimate the **macronutrients** present in the meal, such as calories, proteins, carbohydrates, and fats.

The goal of this project is to help people **track their nutrition easily** without manually entering food data.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 📸 Take a photo of your meal
- 🤖 AI analyzes the food in the image
- 📊 Automatic estimation of macronutrients:
  - Calories
  - Protein
  - Carbohydrates
  - Fats
- 📅 Daily nutrition tracking
- ⚖️ Personalized recommendations based on:
  - weight
  - height
  - daily calorie needs
- 🥗 Meal history tracking to monitor nutrition over time

---

## 🧠 How It Works

The application uses image analysis and external APIs to detect food items and estimate their nutritional values.

The system processes the image, identifies the foods in the meal, and calculates the approximate macronutrients based on nutrition databases.

This allows users to quickly understand what they are eating and track their diet without manual input.
