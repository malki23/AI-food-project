What is CALFOOD ?

Thanks to multiple technology tools, CALFOOD is able to calculate all the exact food informations about your meal and send you all the nutriments you have in your plate like the quantity of protein , lipid , glucide and much more.
Just by taking a single picture and the weight of your plate, CALFOOD already now what's in your plate.
